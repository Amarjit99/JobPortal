## üë§ Account & Registration (Job Seekers)

### Q: How do I create an account?

**A:** 
1. Click "Sign Up" on the homepage
2. Select "Job Seeker" as account type
3. Fill in your name, email, phone number, and password
4. Click "Create Account"
5. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox

---

### Q: Do I need to verify my email?

**A:** Yes, email verification is required to:
- Apply for jobs
- Save jobs
- Receive job alerts
- Ensure account security

Check your spam folder if you don't receive the verification email within 5 minutes.

---

### Q: Is Job Portal free for job seekers?

**A:** Yes! Job Portal is 100% free for job seekers. You can:
- Create a profile
- Upload your resume
- Search unlimited jobs
- Apply to unlimited positions
- Track your applications
- Save jobs for later
- Set up job alerts

No credit card or payment required.

---

### Q: Can I have multiple accounts?

**A:** No, each person should have only one account. Multiple accounts may be flagged and suspended. If you need to change your role (job seeker to recruiter or vice versa), contact support.

---

### Q: How do I delete my account?

**A:**
1. Go to Profile ‚Üí Settings
2. Scroll to "Account Management"
3. Click "Delete Account"
4. Confirm deletion (this is permanent)

**Note:** Once deleted, you cannot recover your account, applications, or saved jobs.

## üìÑ Profile & Resume

### Q: What should I include in my profile?

**A:** For the best results, include:
- Professional photo
- Compelling bio (2-3 sentences)
- Current resume (PDF format)
- 8-15 relevant skills
- Work experience (most recent 3-5 positions)
- Education history
- Certifications (if applicable)

Profiles that are 100% complete get 3x more employer views!

---

### Q: What file formats are supported for resumes?

**A:** Accepted formats:
- PDF (recommended)
- DOC
- DOCX

**Maximum file size:** 5MB

**Tip:** Use PDF format to preserve formatting across all devices.

---

### Q: How do I update my resume?

**A:**
1. Go to your Profile
2. Click "Upload Resume" or click on your existing resume
3. Select new file
4. Click "Save"

Your new resume will be sent with all future applications. Previous applications keep the old resume.

---

### Q: Can employers see my current employer?

**A:** Yes, all information in your profile is visible to employers. If you're currently employed and concerned about privacy:
- Consider not listing your current position
- Or add a note "Currently employed, seeking new opportunities"
- Use a personal email (not work email)

---

### Q: How many skills should I add?

**A:** Recommended: 8-15 skills

**Include:**
- Technical skills specific to your field
- Tools and software you use
- Soft skills (communication, leadership, teamwork)
- Industry-specific knowledge

**Avoid:**
- Generic skills everyone has
- Skills you're not proficient in
- Outdated technologies (unless job requires them)

---

## üîç Job Search

### Q: How do I search for jobs?

**A:** Multiple ways to find jobs:

**Basic Search:**
- Enter keywords in the homepage search bar
- Add location (optional)
- Click Search

**Advanced Filters:**
- Job type (Full-time, Part-time, Contract, Internship)
- Salary range
- Experience level
- Location
- Date posted

**Browse:**
- View all jobs without filters
- Explore by category
- Check featured jobs

---

### Q: What are "Featured Jobs"?

**A:** Featured jobs are:
- Premium job postings from verified employers
- Displayed at the top of search results
- Often from high-quality companies
- Typically reviewed faster by employers

These are excellent opportunities worth applying to!

---

### Q: Can I search for remote jobs?

**A:** Yes! When searching:
1. Enter job keywords
2. In location field, type "Remote"
3. Or use the "Remote" filter
4. View all remote opportunities

---

### Q: How often are new jobs posted?

**A:** New jobs are posted daily! To stay updated:
- Set up job alerts (you'll receive emails when matching jobs are posted)
- Check the "Last 24 hours" filter
- Log in regularly to see new opportunities

---

### Q: Why don't I see jobs in my area?

**A:** Possible reasons:
- No jobs currently match your search in that location
- Try broader location (e.g., state instead of city)
- Expand your search radius
- Look for remote opportunities
- Try different keywords

## üì§ Applications

### Q: How do I apply for a job?

**A:**
1. Find a job you're interested in
2. Click on the job to view details
3. Review requirements and job description
4. Click "Apply Now"
5. Your profile and resume are automatically submitted
6. Receive confirmation message

That's it! No need to fill out lengthy forms for each application.

---

### Q: Can I apply to the same job twice?

**A:** No, you can only apply once per job posting. Make sure your profile and resume are up-to-date before applying!

---

### Q: How do I track my applications?

**A:**
1. Go to Profile ‚Üí Applied Jobs
2. See all jobs you've applied to
3. View status for each application:
   - **Pending:** Under review
   - **Accepted:** Employer interested (expect interview invitation)
   - **Rejected:** Not selected for this position

---

### Q: Can I withdraw an application?

**A:** Yes, if you change your mind:
1. Go to Applied Jobs
2. Find the application
3. Click "Withdraw Application"
4. Confirm

**Note:** This cannot be undone. You cannot reapply to the same job.

---

### Q: How long does it take for employers to respond?

**A:** Response times vary:
- **Typical:** 1-2 weeks
- **Fast:** 3-5 days
- **Slow:** Up to 4 weeks

**Note:** No response doesn't mean rejection. Many employers review applications in batches. You'll be notified of any status changes.

---

### Q: What does "Pending" status mean?

**A:** Pending means:
- Your application was successfully submitted
- Employer has not yet reviewed it, or
- Employer is still reviewing candidates
- No decision has been made yet

Be patient! Keep applying to other opportunities while you wait.

---

### Q: I was rejected. What should I do?

**A:** Don't be discouraged! Job hunting is a numbers game.

**Next steps:**
1. Review your profile - is it complete?
2. Update your resume if needed
3. Apply to more positions
4. Target jobs that closely match your skills
5. Consider improving skills in high-demand areas

Most successful job seekers apply to 50-100 positions before getting hired.

## üíº Job Postings (Recruiters)

### Q: How do I post a job?

**A:**
1. Go to Dashboard ‚Üí Post Job
2. Fill in job details:
   - Job title
   - Description
   - Requirements
   - Salary (optional but recommended)
   - Location
   - Job type
3. Preview your posting
4. Click "Post Job"

Your job goes live immediately!

---

### Q: How long does a job stay active?

**A:** Jobs stay active for:
- **Default:** 30 days
- **Custom:** Set your own deadline
- **Extensions:** Can be extended before expiring

You'll receive reminders before jobs expire.

---

### Q: How much does it cost to post jobs?

**A:** Pricing depends on your plan:

**Free Plan:** 3 active jobs  
**Standard Plan ($99/mo):** 10 active jobs  
**Professional Plan ($199/mo):** Unlimited jobs  
**Enterprise Plan:** Custom pricing

See pricing page for full details.

---

### Q: Can I edit a job after posting?

**A:** Yes!
1. Go to Dashboard ‚Üí My Jobs
2. Find the job
3. Click "Edit"
4. Update information
5. Save changes

Edited jobs are refreshed in search results.

---

### Q: How do I close a job posting?

**A:**
1. Go to My Jobs
2. Find the job
3. Click "Close Job" or toggle to Inactive
4. Job stops appearing in searches

You can still view applications for closed jobs.

---

### Q: Should I include salary information?

**A:** **Highly recommended!** Jobs with salary get:
- 40% more applications
- Better quality candidates
- Faster hiring
- More engaged applicants

Even a range (e.g., "$80k-$100k") helps significantly.

---

### Q: What makes a good job description?

**A:** Effective job descriptions include:
- Clear job title
- Detailed responsibilities (5-7 bullet points)
- Required qualifications (must-haves)
- Preferred qualifications (nice-to-haves)
- Salary range
- Benefits and perks
- Company culture overview
- Application process timeline

Keep it concise (300-700 words) and honest about requirements.

## üîß Technical Issues

### Q: Why can't I log in?

**A:** Common solutions:
1. Check email and password are correct
2. Ensure Caps Lock is off
3. Try "Forgot Password" to reset
4. Clear browser cache and cookies
5. Try a different browser
6. Check if account is suspended (check email)

---

### Q: I didn't receive the verification email

**A:**
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Wait 5-10 minutes
3. Click "Resend Verification Email"
4. Verify email address is correct
5. Add noreply@jobportal.com to contacts
6. Contact support if still not received

---

### Q: My resume won't upload

**A:** Troubleshooting:
1. Check file format (PDF, DOC, DOCX only)
2. File size under 5MB
3. Rename file (remove special characters)
4. Try different browser
5. Convert to PDF
6. Contact support if issue persists

---

### Q: The site is loading slowly

**A:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try different browser
4. Disable browser extensions
5. Check if site is under maintenance (status.jobportal.com)
