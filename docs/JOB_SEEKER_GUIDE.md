## üìù Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Register

1. Visit the Job Portal homepage
2. Click **"Sign Up"** in the top navigation
3. Select **"Job Seeker"** as your account type
4. Fill in the registration form:
   - **Full Name:** Your complete name as it appears on official documents
   - **Email Address:** Use a professional email address
   - **Phone Number:** Include country code (e.g., +1234567890)
   - **Password:** Create a strong password (minimum 6 characters)
   - **Profile Photo:** Upload a professional photo (optional but recommended)

5. Click **"Create Account"**

### Step 2: Verify Your Email

1. Check your email inbox for a verification email
2. Click the verification link in the email
3. Your account will be activated

**Tip:** Check your spam folder if you don't receive the email within 5 minutes.

### Step 3: Complete Your Profile

After email verification, you'll be directed to complete your profile. This is crucial for attracting employers!

## üë§ Setting Up Your Profile

A complete and professional profile increases your chances of getting noticed by employers.

### Basic Information

1. Navigate to **Profile** from the top menu
2. Click **"Edit Profile"**
3. Update the following:
   - **Full Name**
   - **Email** (if needed)
   - **Phone Number**
   - **Bio:** Write a compelling 2-3 sentence summary about yourself

**Example Bio:**
```
Experienced software developer with 5 years of expertise in React and Node.js. 
Passionate about creating user-friendly applications and solving complex problems. 
Seeking opportunities in innovative tech companies.
```

### Professional Photo

- Upload a clear, professional headshot
- Dress professionally
- Use good lighting
- Smile naturally
- Avoid group photos or casual selfies

**Image Requirements:**
- Format: JPG, PNG
- Size: Maximum 5MB
- Recommended: 400x400 pixels or larger

### Skills Section

1. Add relevant skills to your profile
2. Click **"Add Skill"**
3. Enter skill names separated by commas
4. Include both technical and soft skills

**Tips for Skills:**
- Be specific (e.g., "React.js" instead of just "JavaScript")
- Include tools and technologies you've used
- Add soft skills (communication, teamwork, leadership)
- Keep skills relevant to your target jobs
- Aim for 8-15 skills

**Example Skills:**
```
JavaScript, React.js, Node.js, MongoDB, Express.js, Git, Agile, 
Team Collaboration, Problem Solving, Communication
```

### Resume Upload

1. Click **"Upload Resume"** in your profile
2. Select your resume file (PDF recommended)
3. Ensure your resume is:
   - Up-to-date with recent experience
   - Well-formatted and easy to read
   - Free of spelling and grammar errors
   - 1-2 pages in length
   - Saved with a professional filename (e.g., "John_Doe_Resume.pdf")

**Resume Format:** PDF, DOC, DOCX  
**Maximum Size:** 5MB

### Education History

1. Navigate to **Profile ‚Üí Education**
2. Click **"Add Education"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Institution Name**
   - **Degree/Qualification**
   - **Field of Study**
   - **Start Date**
   - **End Date** (or "Present" if ongoing)
   - **Grade/GPA** (optional)
   - **Description** (achievements, relevant coursework)

4. Click **"Save"**

**Tip:** Add all relevant education, starting with the most recent.

### Work Experience

1. Navigate to **Profile ‚Üí Experience**
2. Click **"Add Experience"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Job Title**
   - **Company Name**
   - **Location**
   - **Start Date**
   - **End Date** (or check "Currently working here")
   - **Description:** Detail your responsibilities and achievements

4. Click **"Save"**

**Tips for Experience Descriptions:**
- Use action verbs (developed, managed, implemented)
- Include quantifiable achievements (increased sales by 20%)
- Focus on relevant experience for your target roles
- Keep descriptions concise (3-5 bullet points)

**Example:**
```
Senior Software Developer
Tech Corp Inc. | San Francisco, CA | Jan 2020 - Present

- Developed and maintained 15+ web applications using React and Node.js
- Led a team of 5 developers, improving project delivery time by 30%
- Implemented CI/CD pipeline, reducing deployment time from 2 hours to 15 minutes
- Mentored junior developers through code reviews and pair programming
```

### Certifications

1. Navigate to **Profile ‚Üí Certifications**
2. Click **"Add Certification"**
3. Enter:
   - **Certification Name**
   - **Issuing Organization**
   - **Issue Date**
   - **Expiration Date** (if applicable)
   - **Credential ID/URL** (optional)

4. Click **"Save"**

## üîç Searching for Jobs

### Basic Search

1. Enter keywords in the search bar on the homepage
2. Add location (optional)
3. Click **"Search"** or press Enter

**Search Tips:**
- Use job titles (e.g., "Software Engineer", "Marketing Manager")
- Use skills (e.g., "React", "Digital Marketing")
- Use company names (e.g., "Google", "Microsoft")
- Use industry terms (e.g., "FinTech", "Healthcare")

### Advanced Filters

Refine your search using filters:

**Location:**
- City, state, or region
- Remote work options
- Multiple locations

**Job Type:**
- Full-time
- Part-time
- Contract
- Internship

**Salary Range:**
- Minimum salary requirement
- Salary range filters

**Experience Level:**
- Entry-level (0-2 years)
- Mid-level (3-5 years)
- Senior (5-10 years)
- Expert (10+ years)

**Date Posted:**
- Last 24 hours
- Last 7 days
- Last 30 days

### Viewing Job Details

1. Click on any job listing to view full details
2. Review:
   - Job description
   - Requirements and qualifications
   - Salary information
   - Company information
   - Number of applicants
   - Application deadline

3. Check if you match the requirements
4. Read about the company culture

## üì§ Applying for Jobs

### One-Click Apply

If your profile is complete:

1. Click **"Apply Now"** on the job listing
2. Your profile information and resume are automatically submitted
3. You'll receive a confirmation message
4. The job will appear in your "Applied Jobs" section

### Review Before Applying

**Before clicking apply:**
- ‚úÖ Ensure your resume is up-to-date
- ‚úÖ Verify your contact information is correct
- ‚úÖ Review the job requirements one more time
- ‚úÖ Research the company
- ‚úÖ Prepare for potential follow-up

### Application Status

After applying, your application can have these statuses:

- **Pending:** Application submitted, awaiting review
- **Accepted:** Employer is interested, expect interview invitation
- **Rejected:** Not selected for this position

**Note:** You can only apply once per job posting.

## üíæ Saved Jobs & Alerts

### Saving Jobs

Save interesting jobs to apply later:

1. Click the **"Save"** (bookmark) icon on any job listing
2. Job is added to your Saved Jobs
3. Access saved jobs from **Profile ‚Üí Saved Jobs**

**Benefits:**
- Save jobs for later review
- Compare multiple opportunities
- Build a list of target companies
- Apply when you're ready

### Unsaving Jobs

1. Go to **Saved Jobs**
2. Click **"Remove"** or the bookmark icon again
3. Job is removed from your saved list

### Job Alerts

Set up alerts to get notified of new jobs:

1. Navigate to **Profile ‚Üí Settings**
2. Click **"Job Alert Preferences"**
3. Configure:
   - **Keywords:** Job titles, skills, or industries
   - **Location:** Preferred work locations
   - **Job Type:** Full-time, Part-time, etc.
   - **Frequency:** Daily, Weekly, or Real-time

4. Click **"Save Preferences"**

**You'll receive emails when:**
- New jobs match your criteria
- Saved jobs have updates
- Application status changes

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "I can't log in"

**Solutions:**
1. Check your email and password are correct
2. Ensure Caps Lock is off
3. Try "Forgot Password" to reset
4. Clear browser cache and cookies
5. Try a different browser

#### "I didn't receive the verification email"

**Solutions:**
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Wait 5-10 minutes for delivery
3. Click "Resend Verification Email"
4. Verify email address is correct
5. Add noreply@jobportal.com to contacts
6. Contact support if issue persists

#### "My resume won't upload"

**Solutions:**
1. Check file format (PDF, DOC, DOCX only)
2. Ensure file size is under 5MB
3. Try compressing the file
4. Rename file (remove special characters)
5. Try a different browser
6. Convert to PDF format

#### "Job search shows no results"

**Solutions:**
1. Broaden your search terms
2. Remove some filters
3. Check spelling of keywords
4. Try different job titles
5. Expand location radius
6. Try searching without location

#### "I can't apply for a job"

**Possible Reasons:**
- Profile incomplete (complete all required fields)
- Already applied to this job
- Job posting has expired
- Application deadline passed
- Technical issue (try different browser)

**Solutions:**
1. Complete your profile (especially resume upload)
2. Refresh the page
3. Check application status in Applied Jobs
4. Try again in a few minutes
5. Contact support if error persists

#### "My application status hasn't changed"

**Note:** 
- Employers review applications at their own pace
- Typical review time: 1-2 weeks
- You'll be notified of status changes via email
- No news is not necessarily bad news
